Dear Hysanians,

I am very pleased and excited to warmly invite you to join the Social Club – Higher Level.

This invitation is a special opportunity for those who are ready to grow, challenge themselves, and take their speaking skills to the next level. You have already shown effort, potential, and the ability to improve. Now, it is time to go further.

The Social Club – Higher Level is designed especially to boost your speaking confidence, fluency, and communication skills. In this club, you will:

  • Practice speaking more confidently in front of others
  • Improve your vocabulary and sentence structure
  • Participate in meaningful discussions and debates
  • Share your opinions clearly and respectfully
  • Build leadership and teamwork skills

This is not just about speaking English. It is about finding your voice. It is about learning to express your ideas with confidence. It is about becoming brave enough to speak without fear.

Remember, confidence does not come from being perfect — it comes from practicing, trying, and improving every day. The more you speak, the stronger you become.

The Higher Level Social Club will challenge you, but it will also support you. You will be surrounded by motivated students who want to grow just like you. Together, we will create a positive environment where everyone feels encouraged to speak and improve.

I truly believe that each of you has great potential. This club is your opportunity to unlock it.

You are warmly invited to take this step forward. Be brave. Be confident. Be ready to grow.

I look forward to seeing you shine in the Social Club – Higher Level.

With encouragement and pride,
Tr. Melvin
